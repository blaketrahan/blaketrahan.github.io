Servings: 5

15.25 oz kidney beans
400 g can diced fireroasted tomatoes (about same size can of beans)
6 oz tomato paste
3 sweet potatoes peeled and cut into 1" cubes
1 medium onion, diced
3-5 garlic cloves minced
1 tbsp avocado or canola oil for sauteing onions

1 1/2 tbsp Ancho chili powder
2 tsp ground cumin powder
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp smoked paprika
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon

3/4 cup water or vegetable stock (more than that and it might be too watery)
